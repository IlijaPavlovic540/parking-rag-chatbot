# Parking Rules & Policies

## Vehicle requirements
- Max height: **2.10 m**
- Max width: **2.30 m**
- Electric vehicles (EV) are allowed; charging bays are limited.

## Safety & conduct
- Drive slowly (max 10 km/h).
- Follow one-way signs and floor markings.
- No sleeping/camping in the facility.
- No open flames / smoking.

## Lost ticket / missing plate recognition
- If you lose your ticket, contact support at the intercom near Gate A.
- If plate recognition is used and fails, you may need to provide proof of entry time.

## Cancellations (reservations)
- You can cancel a reservation up to **1 hour before** start time without a fee.
- Late cancellations may be charged partially.

## Privacy
- Reservations require personal details (name, surname, car plate, period).
- Personal data is stored in the SQL database and is not placed in the vector database.
