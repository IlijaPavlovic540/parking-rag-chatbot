# Prices & Payment

> Note: Prices can change. The chatbot will use **dynamic data** (SQL) for the latest prices when available.

## Standard rates
- **First 30 minutes:** €1.50
- **Each additional hour (or part thereof):** €2.50
- **Daily maximum (24h):** €18.00

## Subscriptions (optional)
- **Monthly pass:** €160.00 (valid 30 days)
- **Night pass (18:00–08:00):** €85.00 / month

## Payment methods
- Card (Visa/Mastercard)
- Contactless (Apple Pay / Google Pay)
- Cash at the pay station (coins & notes)

## Receipts / invoices
- Receipts available at the pay station.
- Business invoices available upon request via email (include license plate + date/time).

## Refunds & disputes
- If you believe you were charged incorrectly, contact support with:
  - license plate
  - entry/exit time
  - payment receipt number (if available)
