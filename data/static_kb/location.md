# Parking Location & Access

## Address
**Example Parking Center (EPC)**  
Hauptstraße 12, 90402 Nürnberg, Germany

## How to get here
- **By car:** Follow signs to “Zentrum / Altstadt”, then take the exit “Hauptstraße”.
- **By public transport:** U-Bahn line U1 to “Hauptbahnhof”, then 6 minutes walk.
- **By foot:** Main entrance is on Hauptstraße next to the pharmacy.

## Entrance / Exit
- **Vehicle entrance:** Gate A (Hauptstraße, right lane).
- **Vehicle exit:** Gate B (Parallelstraße).
- **Pedestrian exit:** Stairwell P2 and elevator near Gate A.

## Landmarks
- Opposite: City Library
- Nearby: Central Station (Hauptbahnhof)
