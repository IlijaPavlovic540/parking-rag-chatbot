# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Where is the parking located?
See **Location & Access** for the address and directions.

## Can I enter and exit at any time?
Yes, entry/exit is available **24/7**. Support desk hours are limited.

## What are the prices?
See **Prices & Payment**. If the system is configured with dynamic pricing, the chatbot will use the latest values from the database.

## Can I reserve a space?
Yes. Tell the chatbot: “I want to reserve a parking spot”. It will ask for your details and reservation period.

## Do you store my personal data?
Reservation details are stored in the SQL database. Static information is stored in the vector database. The system includes guardrails to avoid exposing sensitive data.

## Is EV charging available?
Yes, but charging bays are limited and may not be reservable (depends on configuration).

## I have a problem at the gate — what should I do?
Use the intercom near Gate A or contact support during business hours.
