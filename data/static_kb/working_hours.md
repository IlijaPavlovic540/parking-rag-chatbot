# Working Hours & Availability

> Note: Working hours and availability are often **dynamic data**. If configured, the chatbot will query SQL for the latest values.

## Parking access (entry/exit)
- **24/7** vehicle entry and exit.

## Customer support
- Monday–Friday: 08:00–18:00
- Saturday: 10:00–14:00
- Sunday & public holidays: closed

## Peak times (typical)
- Weekdays: 08:00–10:00 and 16:00–18:30
- Saturday: 11:00–15:00

## Availability guidance
- The parking has multiple levels and generally has space available.
- For guaranteed space, use the reservation feature.
